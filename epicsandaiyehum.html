EPICS ( Engineering Projects in Community Service ) :
Project applications catering to local community service needs such as access, abilities, education, outreach etc. Submitted by IEEE member with a mandatory collaboration with a NGO. Local high schools can also be involved.
Basic aims for this initiative are increasing the interest levels in technology and engineering, increasing the IEEE membership base, which allows better connectivity amongst professionals and young engineers and enhancing the community, with whatever help that can be provided.

Funding upto $5000 awarded, with projects accepted throughout the year.
Project disciplines :
• Access and Abilities
• Education and Outreach
• Environment
• Human Services

AIYEHUM ( All IEEE young engineers' humanitarian challenge ) :
ALmost similar to EPICS but little differences :
• Especially for Region -10 ( Asia Pacific )
• Teams of 2-4 mandatory with a collaboration with an NGO.
• No-need of being an IEEE member.

Ideals stay similar :
Identify a community problem, develop the solutions to address them, eventually implement them., with support from IEEE.
